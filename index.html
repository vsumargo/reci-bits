About Reci-Bits

Reci-Bits is a web app designed to search recipe and nutrition information using API's.
How to use:

  • First type ingredients (from your fridge or pantry) into the "What is in your Fridge?" box.
  • Then when you click on the add button (or enter), it will be transferred to another textbox; the searchbox.
  • After you have added all the ingredients, you need to click on the "Get Recipes" button, you will then be presented with 12 food (or drink) menu.
  • When you click on the title or the image of the food (or drink), it will take you to a new page with a detailed recipe of that item.


Please note that searches are optimised with less than 5 ingredients.

Thank you for visiting Reci-Bits, we hope you enjoy the food (or drink)!
